What are we and what do we do?

About Us

R3D Munitions is a 3D-Printing Company which focuses on providing 1:12/6-inch scale weapon accessories for action figures at an affordable price. We are focused on designing and manufacturing these accessories using an FDM 3D-Printer.

In addition to our unwavering commitment to quality, we pride ourselves on being accessible to our customer base. To make our products easily accessible, we have established a presence on two prominent platforms: our dedicated Facebook Page and Shopee, an e-commerce platform. This dual-channel approach allows us to cater to a wide and diverse clientele, ensuring that our meticulously crafted accessories find their way into the hands of action figure enthusiasts and collectors around the world.

Mission

At R3D Munitions, our mission is to revolutionize the world of action figure customization by consistently delivering finely crafted, highly detailed 1:12 and 6-inch scale weapon accessories. We are dedicated to empowering enthusiasts and collectors with the tools they need to elevate their action figure experiences. Our commitment to affordability, innovation, and accessibility drives us to create exceptional products while fostering a vibrant community of like-minded enthusiasts.

Vision

Our vision at R3D Munitions is to be the global leader in the 3D-printed action figure accessory industry. We aspire to continually set new standards of excellence in precision and craftsmanship, pushing the boundaries of what is possible through cutting-edge FDM 3D printing technology. We envision a world where every action figure collector can easily access our meticulously designed accessories, allowing them to enhance and personalize their collections with unparalleled ease and creativity.
